Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated personal data from PlanMyPlate, please send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address or phone number associated with your account.

Steps to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to: backflippedstudios@gmail.com
  2. Use subject: Delete My Account
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your name
    • The email or phone number used to create the account

Once we receive your request, we will confirm and complete the deletion within 7 business days.

Why We Ask for Email or Phone

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized requests, we must verify the identity of the account owner before removing any data.
